Origin and Development

The Indubrasil breed was developed in Brazil during the 1930s, resulting from the crossbreeding between Brahman cattle (Bos indicus) and European breeds such as Holstein, Jersey and Brown Swiss (Bos taurus).

  • Developed in Brazil in the 1930s
  • Hybrid between Brahman and European breeds
  • Adapted to tropical and subtropical climates
  • Officially recognized in 1980
